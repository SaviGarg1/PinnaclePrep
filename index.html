What is Pinnacle Prep?

A student-run, community-driven tutoring initiative run by local Georgia high school students offering free, high-quality academic support to elementary and middle school learners.

Core Values & Goals
  • Total Accessibility: Free tutoring for all students.
  • Empathetic Peer Mentorship: Near-peer tutors who recently succeeded.
  • Academic Confidence: Build growth mindset and resilience.
  • Community Impact: Raise local educational outcomes.
Accessibility Mission

All sessions are 100% free — volunteers donate time and expertise to remove financial barriers and make help available to any student who wants it.

Why students love it

  • Friendly mentors who make learning approachable
  • Short, focused sessions that boost confidence
  • Sessions designed for real curriculum needs

Our Services

Designed for elementary and middle school students — hands-on, personalized, and always free.

Foundational Subject Tutoring

One-on-one or small-group sessions focused on curriculum needs and concept mastery.

Read more

Sessions are tailored to the student’s current level. Tutors use worked examples, scaffolded practice, and quick checks for understanding. Typical topics include arithmetic, pre-algebra, reading comprehension, and foundational science concepts.

Homework Assistance

Guided help to complete assignments while teaching techniques for independent practice.

Read more

Tutors walk through problems with students, modeling thought processes and encouraging students to explain reasoning. Over time, students gain strategies for planning, checking work, and studying efficiently.

Writing, ELA & Math Reinforcement

Focused coaching on writing structure, reading analysis, and step-by-step math problem solving.

Read more

Writing support includes brainstorming, paragraph structuring, and early essay practice. ELA sessions target comprehension strategies and vocabulary. Math reinforcement breaks problems into clear steps and uses visual models where helpful.

What We Teach — In Depth

  • Mathematics: Number sense, arithmetic fluency, fractions, ratios, pre-algebra concepts, early algebra skills, and foundational geometry reasoning.
  • ELA & Writing: Reading strategies (summarize, infer, connect), grammar mechanics, paragraph and essay structure, and vocabulary development.
  • Science: Inquiry-based lessons in life, earth, and physical science with hands-on demos and concept maps to build lasting understanding.
